About

用光影记录
时间的痕迹

漫漫人生路，瞎搞搞如何

Born and wandering through the cities of China, I find beauty in the overlooked — the old man with a cigarette, the child chasing pigeons, the woman waiting for rain to stop. Street photography is my way of bearing witness.

过去十年，我走访了北京、上海、重庆、成都、香港等数十座城市，用胶片与数码记录下城市变迁中的人与情感。作品曾入选多个国内外摄影展览。
